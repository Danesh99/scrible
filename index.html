About Micro Computers

MICRO COMPUTERS, known for accurate and reliable translation, was started by Mr.V.Padmanaban, B.Com., in the year 1993. The Company does Legal Translation (Tamil to English) of documents concerning the HIGH COURT & SUPREME COURT. We translate legal documents for the VVIPs and Senior Advocates in Tamil Nadu practising in the HIGH COURT & SUPREME COURT. Legal documents for the seniormost Advocates in the Supreme Court are translated through senior Advocates in Chennai. Besides, we also translate documents pertaining to criminal and civil cases for the litigants and common man as well.

  • Over 32 years of experience
  • Expert team of translators and DTP professionals
  • Comprehensive legal document services
